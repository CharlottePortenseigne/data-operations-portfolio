# 📊 Customer Behavior Insights

## 🧠 Core Question

Who actually drives revenue after conversion?

---

## 💰 Insight 1 — Revenue Comes from Volume

- 86.8% of users are low-value  
- They generate 62.8% of revenue  

👉 Revenue is distributed across many users  

---

## 🔁 Insight 2 — Retention Drives Revenue

- 92% of users are repeat buyers  
- 95% of revenue comes from them  

👉 Without retention, revenue collapses  

---

## 🔄 Insight 3 — Strong Engagement

- 72.2% of users are frequent buyers  

👉 Customers are highly engaged and return regularly  

---

## 💸 Insight 4 — Low Transaction Value Model

- Average purchase: $4.55  
- Median purchase: $2.62  

👉 Many small purchases → high volume model  

---

## ⚠️ Insight 5 — Data Quality Shapes Strategy

- category_code mostly missing  
- brand partially missing  

👉 Focus shifts from product analysis → behavioral analysis  

---

## 🧠 Final Insight

This business operates as a:

👉 **behavior-driven revenue system**

Not driven by:
- high spenders  
- large transactions  

But by:
- repeat behavior  
- frequency  
- customer volume  

---

## 🚀 Strategic Takeaway

To grow revenue:

- Increase retention  
- Increase purchase frequency  
- Convert low-value users into repeat customers  

👉 Not just acquire more users
